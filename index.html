Privacy Policy & Terms
MY EXPENSE LEDGER · JUNE 2026 — Updated
⚠ This policy has been updated. Please read and re-agree to continue.

1. Your Data & Privacy

All financial data you enter is stored in your personal Firebase account under a unique private ID. It is completely private. No other user can access your data. Your phone number is stored as a one-way hash that cannot be reversed or read by anyone.

2. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, protected by security rules ensuring only you can read or write your data. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party. Data is separated by expenses and income modules and stored per monthly period.

3. Authentication & PIN Security

You are identified by your phone number hash and PIN. Your PIN is hashed before storage using a one-way algorithm and is never stored in plain text — not even the app developer can read it. The app uses a PIN pad for daily login. For added security, a periodic full identity check (username/phone + PIN) is triggered randomly every 1 to 2 weeks.

4. Session Security & Inactivity Lock

The app automatically locks to the PIN screen after 10 minutes of inactivity. If you return within 10 minutes the app resumes without requiring a PIN. Logging out clears all local session data immediately. The app never stores your PIN in a readable form on your device.

5. Usage Analytics

This app uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics such as how many people open the app, which country they are in, and what device they use. No personal financial data is ever sent to Google Analytics. No names, phone numbers, or transaction details are tracked. Analytics data is used only by the app developer to understand app usage and improve the experience.

6. No Advertising

This app contains no advertisements. We do not use your financial data for advertising or marketing purposes of any kind.

7. Recycle Bin & Data Deletion

Deleted entries are moved to a Recycle Bin before permanent deletion. You can restore them anytime. A Full Reset option wipes all local device data while preserving your cloud backup in Firebase. Your cloud data is never deleted without your explicit action.

8. Contact

If you have concerns about your data, use the Help button inside the app to contact support at myfinanceledgerapp@gmail.com.
